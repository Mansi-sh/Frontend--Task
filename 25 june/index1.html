BLOOD DONATION

Blood saves millions of lives across the world in a variety of situations. Millions of blood units are collected from donors and still many millions are required to be collected to meet the requirement of the world [1]. By donating the blood, helps a needy and save a precious life. The information of the blood donor is required when there is need of blood The average human body contains about five liters of blood, which is made of several cellular and non-cellular components such as Red blood cell, Platelet, and Plasma. Each type of component has its unique properties and can be used for different indications. The donated blood is separated into these components by the blood centre and one donated unit can save upto four lives depending on the number of components separated from your blood.
